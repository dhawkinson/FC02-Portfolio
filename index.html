Allow Me To Introduce Myself

I’m an energetic developer who aspires to master the web. I am also a seasoned IT professional with more than 25 years bringing technology solutions to the healthcare industry. After a flirtation with retirement, I am reentering the marketplace ... feeling unproductive was not for me. With a new beginning, a new direction seemed appropriate, so I decided to do what has given me the most gratification; developing solutions with software. Nothing is more gratifying than putting a working solution in the hands of a grateful user.

I'm not merely about work. My wife and I can be found worshipping together most Sunday mornings. Our two Australian Shepherds are a blessing and a kick. Of course that entails fetch, walks and dog parks. For music; I like Classic Rock, Country (newer), Jazz and Classical. For reading; it's the Bible, theology, philosophy and biographies, and sometimes poetry and novels. On the activity side it's; hiking, bicycling, skiing, camping, auto racing (as a spectator) and international travel.
